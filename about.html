ABOUT US

Our Story

One day, one jump and two years of passion...

After jumping off the side of a cliff with only a cord attached in the Swiss Alps, our founder couldn't go back to his day job. Instead, he set out to create something that he couldn't find in the travel market - A collection of customizable adventure itineraries that are rewarding to the traveler, host and the environment. Adventure agency is the end result of two years of full time travel in search of the best adventure experiences in the Carpathians has to offer.

Our Values

Journeys worth taking

We prefer to work with locally owned and operated lodging and tour operators. This allows more money to stay inside of local communities and in the hands of people who live in the destinations you visit.

We give 10% of income to conservation and charity organizations.

We strive to provide meaningful, rewarding and challenging adventures for our clients with the highest level of consultation services.

We believe in integrity, both to our partners and our clients. We ask for reasonable commissions from our partners and provide transparency in pricing for travelers.

Our Service Categories

Consult & Book

We help you understand product offerings from our carefully selected supplier base. We book lodging and scheduled group tours as well as related transfers and logistics where necessary

Adventure Itineraries

We create unique itineraries by combining lodging, transportation, equipment and experiences and handling complex logistics. These itineraries can be self guided or privately guided and can be tailored to suit your desires. This service will provide you with a personalized itinerary, guide / guidebooks, maps and all info to get you from A to Z for you to enjoy everything in between.

From Scratch

You bring us an idea and we turn it into reality. We draw your exclusive journey from scratch including complex logistics, experiences, guides and/or guidebooks, lodging, equipment, and itinerary. Tell us your epic idea, and will make it happen.
